Olivia Luan

Pianist | Musician | Performer

About

Olivia Luan is a talented 15-year-old musician who began her musical journey at the age of four. She currently studies piano with Markus Pawlik and has achieved outstanding success in numerous competitions.

Achievements

  • Second Prize - 2024 United States Open Music Competition Showcase Piano Solo
  • First Prize - 2023 Crescendo International Music Competition (Carnegie Hall Performance)
  • First Prize - 2024 US International Open Music Competition
  • Silver Award - 2021 International Young Artists Competition

Performances

Olivia will be performing at the Bankhead Theater on February 15, 2025, as a winner of the 52nd Annual Competition for Young Musicians.
